G-value Calculator

In the SPD framework, the G-value represents the gravitational coupling strength associated with a specific element, isotope, molecule, or material composition.

This calculator allows users to explore and calculate predicted G-value variations for elements, isotopes, molecules, mixed materials, and interacting masses.

All displayed G-values are expressed in units of 10⁻¹¹ m³ kg⁻¹ s⁻².

GX = GH × (npXmp + nnXmn) / mX

GX = G-value of element X

GH = G-value of hydrogen

npX = number of protons

nnX = number of neutrons

mp = mass of proton

mn = mass of neutron

mX = inertial mass of element X
